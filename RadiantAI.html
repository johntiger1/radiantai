Accelerated workflow and increased accuracy with AI-powered radiology solutions.

RadiantAI combines cutting-edge AI and NLP solutions with data from multiple medical sources to provide clinicians with an advanced radiology workflow. Our proprietary technology is designed to detect patterns, identify anomalies, and reduce misdiagnoses while allowing radiologists to work faster and more efficiently. 2

Reduce Misdiagnoses with AI-Powered Radiology

RadiantAI leverages advanced machine learning and natural language processing to analyze imaging data, unstructured medical reports, and vital sign measurements. Our AI-powered radiology platform aims to reduce misdiagnoses by surfacing cases with high levels of complexity or discrepancies that require additional attention from radiologists.

3

Improved radiologist workflows and accuracy of diagnosis.

RadiantAI utilizes an innovative approach of combining multimodal text and vital signs data from Electronic Medical Records (EMR) with AI technology to improve radiologists’ workflows and accuracy of diagnosis. Our proprietary research enables us to accurately interpret a variety of types of imaging studies, including CT scans, MRIs, X-rays, ultrasounds without the need for additional tests or manual annotation.
